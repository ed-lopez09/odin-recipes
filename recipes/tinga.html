Chicken Tinga Tostadas

Description

Another delicious mexican dish that's super easy to make and doesn't take long to make at all! Made with some chipotle sauce to add a little heat to the chicken as well as extra flavor. It's traditionally topped with just some crema and some shredded queso blanco, but feel free to add more toppings. Can't really make a bad decision! I'll be adding some shredded lettuce and avocado to mine, but you're not limited to just what I put here. Feel free to experminent. Now we'll get into the ingredients list.

Ingredients

  • Rotisserie chicken
  • tostadas
  • 7 roma tomatoes
  • half a white onion
  • 7 oz can of chipotle in adobo sauce
  • chicken bouillon
  • shredded lettuce
  • block of queso blanco
  • crema
  • OPTIONAL: avocado
With our ingredients, we're now ready to start!
